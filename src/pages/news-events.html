CTC (Creative Technology Commons)

The Creative Technology Commons at Auraria Library acts as a convergent space for digital teaching, learning, and research. The Commons facilitates access to hardware, software, and hands-on guidance in the use of media and digital technologies.

Ask Us Desk

Walk-up help - Please visit the Ask Us Desk in the main lobby for assistance. Ideally, walk-up help is for quick questions that will likely take 15 minutes or less to answer

Writing Center

The Writing Center is a collaborative, tri-institutional resource that provides one-on-one and small-group assistance to undergraduate and graduate students from any school on the Auraria Campus at the new Auraria Library location.
